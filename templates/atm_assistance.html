Learn how to use an ATM safely and confidently in your preferred language

How to Use an ATM

Step 1: Insert Your Card

Insert your ATM or debit card into the card slot with the chip facing up and towards the machine. Ensure the card is inserted correctly as shown on the machine.

Step 2: Select Language

Choose your preferred language (e.g., English, Hindi, Kannada) from the options displayed on the screen for easier navigation.

Step 3: Enter PIN

Enter your 4-digit PIN using the keypad. Cover the keypad with your hand to keep your PIN private and secure.

Step 4: Choose Transaction

Select the desired transaction, such as Cash Withdrawal, Balance Enquiry, or PIN Change, from the menu on the screen.

Step 5: Complete Transaction

For cash withdrawal, enter the amount and collect your cash. Take your card and receipt (if selected) before leaving the ATM.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if my card gets stuck in the ATM?

Do not try to force the card out. Contact the bank’s helpline number displayed on the ATM or visit the nearest branch immediately to report the issue.

What if I forget my PIN?

Visit your bank branch or use the bank’s mobile app or website to reset your PIN. You may need to verify your identity with documents like Aadhaar or PAN.

Is it safe to use an ATM at night?

Use ATMs in well-lit, secure locations. Avoid isolated ATMs at night and ensure no one is watching you enter your PIN.
